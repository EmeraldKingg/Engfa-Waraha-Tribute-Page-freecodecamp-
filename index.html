Engfa Waraha

Engfa Waraha is a Thai musician and beauty pageant titleholder.

She appeared in the seventh season of the Voice Thailand In 2022, she won Miss Grand Thailand 2022 representing Bangkok. She represented Thailand at Miss Grand International 2022 held in Indonesia on 25 October, and was first runner up.

Here's a time line of Engfa Waraha's life:

  • 1995 - Waraha was born in Uthai Thani province into a family of musicians; her father was a keyboardist and her mother was a luk thung (Thai pop-country) singer.
  • 2022 - In 2022, she won Miss Grand Thailand 2022 representing Bangkok. She represented Thailand at Miss Grand International 2022 held in Indonesia on 25 October, and was first runner up
  • 2013 - Later in 2013, a year after her father's death, she had the opportunity to meet with a master of the Thai blessing ceremony "Phonraphee Maneethai" (Thai: พรระพี มณีไทย) who later persuaded her to study to be an officiant skill, as well as a Thai verse singing (Thai: ขับเสภา) until proficient enough to be able to use it for a career.
  • 2018 - Waraha became known as "Engfa Sangravee" after participating in the seventh season of the reality singing contest The Voice Thailand in 2018. She was eliminated in the knockout round, of the second phase of the contest.
  • 2020 - In 2020, Waraha entered the Miss Grand Suphanburi [th] contest, a provincial preliminary pageant for Miss Grand Thailand, but withdraw due to contractual problems.
  • 2022 - She returned to pageantry in 2022 and won Miss Grand Bangkok 2022.
  • 2015At the age of 17, due to her father's terminal cancer, she signed a 12-year contract with the Bangkok-based artist management organ "Sangravee Entertainment" to pay for her father's cancer treatment,and released four singles; two in 2015 and the rest in 2016
  • 2016 - In addition, before entering the national entertainment industry, she also worked as a waitress, greengrocer, food vendor, caddie, and model.
